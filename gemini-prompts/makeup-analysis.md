## The Prompt

```
Analyze this image. Use a red pen to mark areas that can be improved.
```

## Input

Upload a portrait or selfie showing the makeup you want feedback on.

## What It Analyzes

- Eyebrow shape and symmetry
- Eye makeup blending and placement
- Foundation coverage and matching
- Lip liner and lipstick application
- Contouring and highlighting
- Overall color harmony
