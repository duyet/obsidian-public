Convert any image into a chalk illustration on a Japanese classroom blackboard.

![Example](https://github.com/PicoTrex/Awesome-Nano-Banana-images/raw/main/images/pro_case28/output.jpg)

## The Prompt

```
Draw this illustration with colored chalk on a blackboard, the blackboard is
shot from an angle, the scene is from a Japanese classroom, the blackboard
is placed against the wall, the teacher's desk is in front, and "Tiger is
coming!" is written in chalk next to the illustration.
```

## Input

Upload a reference image to be drawn as chalk art.

## Customization

- Change the text next to the illustration
- Adjust the classroom setting (Western classroom, lecture hall, etc.)
- Modify the camera angle (straight-on, from below, etc.)
